About Me #

I am Zhengbo Qu, an undergraduate Mathematics major at Peking University with passion for mathematical research.

I am interested in a range of fields, including dynamical systems, low-dimensional topology, geometric group theory and hyperbolic geometry. I am fascinated by the deep connections between these fields, particularly how dynamical techniques illuminate problems in topology and group theory.

In the section Research, I provide a brief overview of my current research project.

In the section Notes, I share some of my notes on various topics in mathematics, some of which are based on final projects for courses I have taken and some are based on my talks at seminars.
